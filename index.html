The Challenge

Why Traditional AI Fails

🔴 No Governance

AI decisions happen in black boxes with no accountability or policy enforcement. Drift happens, and you can't trace why.

🔴 Fragmented Memory

Each agent operates in isolation. No shared context, no learning from past decisions, no evolving knowledge base.

🔴 No Scalability

Adding agents means chaos. No coordination, no conflict resolution, no way to scale beyond a handful of agents.

Sunflower + Honeycomb

Scalability + Efficiency + Connection

Sunflower governance provides policy-driven execution with infinite scalability. Honeycomb departments enable efficient cross-hive collaboration and clear organizational boundaries. Together, they form the foundation of Daena's architecture.

Sunflower Governance

Scalability

Policy-driven execution with auditable, traceable decision-making. ABAC tiers, Merkle-notarized lineage, and auto threat detection. Prevents drift and enforces accountable autonomy at scale.

Honeycomb Departments

Efficiency + Connection

Agents organized as departments for scalable execution and cross-hive collaboration. Clear boundaries, measurable outputs, and efficient resource allocation.

Agent Swarms
