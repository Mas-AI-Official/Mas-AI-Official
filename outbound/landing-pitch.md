## The three questions every prospect asks

**Q1: "How is this different from hiring a solo pen tester on Upwork?"**

> Two things. First: I built the AI security platform I audit with —
> most contractors are bolting Burp + custom scripts on top of training
> data. I know the attack surface by construction. Second: I ship you
> the code. When I find something, the fix is architected by me with
> Daena's governance wrapper. You don't have to pay another vendor to
> remediate what the audit found.

**Q2: "Why should I trust you with my security?"**

> Three reasons. One: the same Klyntar platform I use to audit you is
> what protects my own production. Two: I ship a proof-of-exploit bundle
> — if I can't break it, I say so. Three: I don't retain customer data
> or credentials past the engagement. Asset Shield is production-grade;
> vault material is destroyed in 24 hours post-delivery.

**Q3: "How fast can you start?"**

> Free recon: 48 hours from you sending me the URL. Full audit: 5
> business days from scope sign-off. Architect + build: 3 weeks from
> signature. I don't overbook. If I say yes to a slot, it happens.

## Internal rules

- **Never promise a finding before the recon.** If I say "we'll find X"
  and don't, it burns trust.
- **Never fabricate a testimonial.** If I don't have one, I don't pretend.
- **Free-value offer is real.** If someone says yes to the free recon, it
  happens in 48 hours. Block the time the moment they reply.
- **If I can't find something material, I say so out loud.** That builds
  the reputation faster than a fabricated finding.
- **Pipeline review every Friday.** Sent / replied / booked / closed.
